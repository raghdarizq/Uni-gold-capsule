frequently asked questions

What is a capsule hotel?

A capsule hotel is a type of accommodation that provides compact sleeping spaces called "capsules." These capsules are typically stacked in multiple tiers and are designed to provide basic amenities for a short stay, primarily targeting budget-conscious travelers.

Are capsule hotels only for single travelers?

Capsule hotels are primarily designed to accommodate single travelers, but some capsule hotels also offer double occupancy capsules for couples or friends traveling together.

What are the duties of staff at a capsule hotel?

The duties of staff at a capsule hotel include reception and check-in, housekeeping, security, guest services, maintenance, monitoring, and operational tasks.

How do capsule hotels ensure guest privacy?

Capsule hotels ensure guest privacy by providing individual capsules that are equipped with curtains or blinds for added privacy. Additionally, the staff respects guests' privacy and maintains a quiet and peaceful environment within the hotel.
